Coding

As the videos were watched, properties appearing in the videos were noted. These properties were then grouped into four main categories:

  • Visual Aids are visual elements in the video that help the viewer follow along.
  • Presenting Information are various ways videos demonstrated their topic, such as by showing an interface of a program, or showing lines of code.
  • Video Qualities are qualities of the video itself, such as having audio or HD video quality.
  • Supplemental Material are extra material found on the website hosting the video.
